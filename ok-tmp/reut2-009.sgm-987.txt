FED SETS TWO BILLION DLR CUSTOMER REPURCHASE

    NEW YORK, March 26 - 

The Federal Reserve entered the U.S. government securities market to arrange two billion dlrs of customer repurchase agreements, a spokeswoman for the New York Fed said.     Federal funds were trading at 6-3/16 pct at the time of the indirect injection of temporary reserves.  Reuter &#3;
