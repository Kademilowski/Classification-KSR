DEXTER <DEX> UNIT TO LICENSE ADHESIVES

    PITTSBURG, Calif., March 26 - 

Dexter Corp said its Hysol Aerospace and Industrial Products division signed an agreement to license its engineering adhesives to Toyoda Gosei, an affiliate of <Toyota Motor Co> of Japan.     Dexter said technology exchanges will begin within the next three months and both partners will work together to develop a complete line of structural adhesive and application techniques for the automotive and other industries.
