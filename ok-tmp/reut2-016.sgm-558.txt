CENTRAL BANKERS HOLDS REGULAR BIS MONTHLY MEETING

    BASLE, Switzerland, April 13 - 

Leading central bank governors held their routine monthly meeting at the Bank for International Settlements (BIS) here today, monetary sources said.     There was no indication of subjects discussed. No statements were planned.  Reuter &#3;
