SAUDI ARABIA BUYS 5,000 TONNES RBD PALM OLEIN

    LONDON, March 11 - 

Saudi Arabia bought 5,000 tonnes of refined bleached deodorised palm olein at its import tender yesterday for April 16/25 shipment at 353 dlrs per tonne cost and freight Jeddah, traders said.  REUTER &#3;
