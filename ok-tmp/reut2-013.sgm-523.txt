BANK OF JAPAN INTERVENES SHORTLY AFTER TOKYO OPENS

    TOKYO, April 7 - 

The Bank of Japan bought a small amount of dollars at around 145.95 yen shortly after the Tokyo market opened, dealers said.     But the dollar then fell on speculative selling by trading houses, they said.     The dollar had opened here at 145.95 yen against 145.85/90 yen in New York and 146.00 yen at the close here yesterday.  REUTER &#3;
