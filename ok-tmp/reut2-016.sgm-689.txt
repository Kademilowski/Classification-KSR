HARMON INDUSTRIES <HRMN.O> IN PACT WITH AMTRAK

    KANSAS CITY, Mo., April 13 - 

Harmon Industries said it signed a contract worth more than three mln dlrs with the National Railroad Passenger Corp (Amtrak).     Harmon Industries said the contract includes the design, engineering, manufacturing, delivery and system integration for signalling equipment along Amtrak's 55-mile Philadelphia to Atlantic City, N.J., line.
