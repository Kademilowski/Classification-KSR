KANSAS POWER AND LIGHT <KAN> CHAIRMAN LEAVING

    TOPEKA, Kan., March 25 - 

Kansas Power and Light Co said it will name a successor on or before May 5 to its outgoing chairman and chief executive officer William Wall.     Wall, who will assume the position of chairman and chief executive officer at Asbestos Claims Facility, will fill the positions which have been vacant for several months, the company said.
