KROGER <KR> SAYS UNION REJECTS CONTRACT

    DENVER, June 2 - 

Kroger Co's King Soopers Supermarkets unit said employees, members of the United Food and Commercial Workers Union, rejected its latest contract proposal by a 58 pct to 42 pct margin.     The company said employees have been on strike since May 8, but it has kept the stores open and fully staffed.  Reuter &#3;
