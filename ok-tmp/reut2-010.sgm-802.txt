PANAMA SUGAR VESSEL SAFELY DOCKED AT GREENOCK

    LONDON, March 30 - 

The Panamanian motor vessel Northern 1, 4,217 dwt, was safely towed into Greenock over the weekend after having its crankshaft broken off the Scottish coast during severe weather, Lloyds Shipping Intelligence said.     Northern 1 was loaded with 3,000 tons of sugar from Demerara.  Reuter &#3;
