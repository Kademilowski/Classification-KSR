GENOVA <GNVA> TO MERGE INTO GENOVA PRODUCTS

    DAVISON, Mich., March 30 - 

Genova Inc said its shareholders approved a merger into <Genova Products Inc>, which will be consumated within a few days. Under the agreement, Genova said each of its shareholders will receive 5.375 dlrs per share in cash.  Reuter &#3;
