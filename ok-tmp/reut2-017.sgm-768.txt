BARLEY SOLD TO SAUDI ARABIA  - USDA

    WASHINGTON, June 1 - 

Private exporters reported sales of 150,000 tonnes of barley to Saudi Arabia for delivery in the 1987/88 season, the U.S. Agriculture Deapartment said.     The 1987/88 season for barley begins today.  Reuter &#3;
