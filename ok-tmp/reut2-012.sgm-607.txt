SUN <SUN> RAISES HEATING OIL BARGE PRICE

    NEW YORK, April 2 - 

Sun Co's Sun Refining and Marketing Co subsidiary said it raised the price it charges contract barge customers for heating oil in ny harbor by 0.50 cent a gallon, effective today.     The 0.50 cent price hike brings Sun's heating oil price to 50.75 cts a gallon, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
