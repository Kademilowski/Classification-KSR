MANAGEMENT SCIENCE <MSAI> LOSS MAY TOP 20 CTS

    ATLANTA, April 28 - 

Management Science America Inc, clarifying statements made earlier today, said its loss for the first quarter could exceed 20 cts a share because of non-recurring expenses associated with the acquisition of several companies, including Comserv Inc.     Earlier today, the company told a meeting of investors here that the first quarter loss would be 20 cts a share.      Reuter &#3;
