DOLLAR FALLS BELOW 143 YEN IN TOKYO

    TOKYO, April 10 - 

The dollar fell below 143 yen in hectic early Tokyo trading despite aggressive Bank of Japan intervention, dealers said.     After opening at a Tokyo low of 143.75 yen, the dollar fell as low as 142.90 yen on heavy selling led by securities firms and institutional investors, they said.  REUTER &#3;
