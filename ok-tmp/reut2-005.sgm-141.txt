BANGLADESH TO BUY 10,000 TONNES SOYABEAN OIL

    DHAKA, March 14 - 

Bangladesh floated an international tender for the purchase of 10,000 tonnes of refined soyabean oil for delivery at Chittagong/Chalna port by April 24, Food Ministry officials said.     The tender closes March 28 at 0500 GMT.  REUTER &#3;
