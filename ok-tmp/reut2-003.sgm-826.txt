DATAREX <DRX> TO SELL 3M <MMM> COMPUTER GEAR

    BUFFALO, N.Y., March 11 - 

Datarex Systems Inc said it was named a distributor for Minnesota Mining and Manufacturing Co's data recording media and other computer suppliers and accessories in the United States.     The company said sales of the 3M products will have a material impact on its sales for the balance of 1987.  Reuter &#3;
