SUPERMARKETS GENERAL <SGC> FEBRUARY SALES RISE

    WOODBRIDGE, N.J., March 5 - 

Supermarkets General Corp reported sales of 424.8 mln dlrs for the four-week period ended Feb 28, 1987, A 7.2 pct increase over sales of 396.4 mln dlrs for the comparable period last year.  Reuter &#3;
