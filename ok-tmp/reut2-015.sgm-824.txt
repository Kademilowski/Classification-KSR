MINNESOTA LASER INTRODUCES NEW SURGICAL LASERS

    ST. PAUL, April 9 - 

Minnesota Laser Corp said it introduced a new line of Coagulase Series Nd:YAG surgical laser systems.     It said the lasers use 400 micron fiberoptics designed for endoscopic, microscopic and freehand surgical applications in gynecology, urology, gastroenterology and pulmonology.  Reuter &#3;
