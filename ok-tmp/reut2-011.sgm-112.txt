NEOAX <NOAX> BUYS REXNORD <REX> UNIT

Neoax Inc said it bought Rexnord Inc's Fairfield Manufacturing Co for 70.5 mln dlrs cash.     The unit makes custom gears for industrial use and had sales of 84 mln dlrs in its Oct. 31, 1986 fiscal year.  Reuter &#3;
