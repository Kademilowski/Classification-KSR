SMOKY RIVER SIGNS COAL SUPPLY AGREEMENT

    Calgary, Alberta, March 16 - 

Smoky River Coal Ltd, operator of the Smoky River coal mine near Grande Cache, Alberta, said it agreed to supply 1.5 metric tons of metallurgical coal in the next five years to Pohang Iron and Steel Co of Korea.     Smoky River said the agreement is an extension of the current long-term coal supply contract.  Reuter &#3;
