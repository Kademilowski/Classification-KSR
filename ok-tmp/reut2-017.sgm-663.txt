FRENCH AID TO TOGO TO HELP COFFEE, COCOA TREES

    LOME, June 1 - 

France is to provide Togo with 475 mln cfa francs of aid for a range of projects that include development of the coffee and cocoa industries and reafforestation in the south of the country, official sources said.  Reuter &#3;
