FOREST CITY <FECA> COMPLETES SALE

    CLEVELAND, March 13 - 

Forest City Enterprises Inc said it completed the previously announced sale of assets of its retail store division, excluding real estate, to Handy Andy Home Improvement Centers Inc, a private Gurnee, Ill., firm.     The sale is for cash and notes but exact terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
