STERIVET <STVT> GETS SUPPORT FOR DRUG PROJECT

    MISSISSAUGA, Ontario, March 5 - 

Sterivet Laboratories Limited said the the National Research Council of Canada is contributing financing for the development a new drug to treat a disease of the feet in horses.     It said the drug has produced promising results in limited clinical trials over the past 18 months in Ontario.  Reuter &#3;
