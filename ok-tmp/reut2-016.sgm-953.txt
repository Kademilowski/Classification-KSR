BANK OF JAPAN BUYS MODERATE DOLLAR SUM -- DEALERS

    TOKYO, April 21 - 

The Bank of Japan intervened in the market to try to accelerate the dollar's rise after heavy dollar buying by institutional investors, dealers said.     The central bank bought a moderate amount of dollars at around 142.10 yen, they said.  REUTER &#3;
