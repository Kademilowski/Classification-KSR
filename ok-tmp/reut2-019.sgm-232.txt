DAXOR <DAXR.O> IN FEDERAL EXPRESS <FDX> DEAL

    NEW YORK, June 19 - 

Daxor Corp said it has received an offer from Federal Express Corp to establish a national frozen blood bank at Federal's Memphis, Tenn., Headquarters.     The company said its blood could be shipped anywhere in the U.S. Within six hours from Memphis via Federal Express' delivery system.  REUTER &#3;
