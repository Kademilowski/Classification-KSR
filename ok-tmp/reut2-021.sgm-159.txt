INFOTRON <INFN.O> NAMES NEW CHIEF EXECUTIVE

    CHERRY HILL, N.J., Oct 19 - 

Infotron Systems Corp said James C. Castle has been named president and chief executive officer, succeeding James C. Hann, who becomes vice chairman and will focus on product technology issues.     Castle had been president of TBG Information Systems Inc, a subsidiary of Thyssen AG <THYH.G>.  Reuter &#3;
