MCDONNELL DOUGLAS <MD>, SIEMENS SIGN MEMORANDUM

    NEW YORK, June 18 - 

McDonnell Douglas Corp and Siemens AG <SIEG.F> said they signed a memorandum of understanding dealing with the AH-64 Apache helicopter.     They said the memorandum deals with depot maintenance of the U.S. Army's helicopters based in West Germany and a joint study of the potential development and production of the helicopter for the West German Army.
