ANADARKO <APC> FILES TO OFFER CONVERTIBLE DEBT

    NEW YORK, March 26 - 

Anadarko Petroleum Corp said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 100 mln dlr issue of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012.     Proceeds will be used for the partial repayment of outstanding indebtedness, Anadarko said.     The company named Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc as underwriter of the offering.  Reuter &#3;
