THAI TIN EXPORTS RISE IN JANUARY

    BANGKOK, March 5 - 

Thailand exported 1,816 tonnes of tin metal in January, up from 1,731 in December and 1,330 a year ago, the Mineral Resources Department said.     It said major buyers last month were Singapore, Japan, Britain, the Netherlands, Malaysia and the U.S.  REUTER &#3;
