CATTLE ON FEED REPORT CALLED NEGATIVE

    CHICAGO, April 24 - 

Chicago Mercantile Exchange floor traders' immediate comments on the 13-state USDA quarterly cattle on feed report were negative.     Traders said that placements on feed and total on-feed numbers were at the high end of expectations and will likely weigh on futures tomorrow.  Reuter &#3;
