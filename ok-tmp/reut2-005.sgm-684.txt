FARAH <FRA> NAMES FINANCIAL OFFICER

    EL PASO, Texas, March 16 - 

Farah Manufacturing Co said it named Christopher Carameros senior vice president of finance, effective April 1.     Carameros will succeed Richard Jaeger as chief financial officer. Jaeger, who continues as chief financial officer on a transitional basis, was named vice chairman responsible for corporate finance.     Carameros has been in public accounting for 12 years, most recently as partner at Coopers and Lybrand.  Reuter &#3;
