PRISM ENTERTAINMENT <PRZ> TO SELL VIDEO IN JAPAN

    LOS ANGELES, March 18 - 

Prism Entertainment Corp said it has entered an acquisition and distribution agreement with Tokyo-based Gaga Communications to make and distribute home video titles in Japan under the Prism label.     Both companies will provide funding for joint acquisitions of up to ten mln dlrs.     Gaga is a subsidiary of Nihon Tape Corp, Japan's largest wholesaler of pre-recorded cassettes.     Gaga will manufacture and distribute the videos for the Japanese market.  Reuter &#3;
