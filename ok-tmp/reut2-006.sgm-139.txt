CMS ADVERTISING NEGOTIATES CANADIAN VENTURE

    OKLAHOMA CITY, March 17 - 

<CMS Adversiting Inc> said it is in preliminary discussions with a Canadian group with "extensive franchise experience" for a joint venture to start up a majority-owned CMS Canadian subsidiary called CMA Advertising International of Canada.     CMS also said the group intends to seek an undertaker to investigate a possible stock offering ot the subsidiary.  Reuter &#3;
