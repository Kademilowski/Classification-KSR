CONRAC <CAX> URGES NO ACTION ON BID

    STAMFORD, Conn., March 24 - 

Conrac Corp said it is asking shareholders to take no action on the 25-dlr-per-share tender offer for all its shares launched this morning by Mark IV Industries Inc <IV>.     The company said its board will study the offer with financial and legal advisors and make a recommendation to shareholders by April 17.  Reuter &#3;
