SOUTH KOREA BUYS 50,000 TONNES CANADIAN WHEAT

    WINNIPEG, April 21 - 

South Korea yesterday bought 50,000 tonnes of Canadian feed wheat for late June/early July shipment at 95.00 dlrs per tonne FOB Vancouver, trade sources said.  Reuter &#3;
