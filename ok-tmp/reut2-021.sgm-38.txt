DURAKON <DRKN.O> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

    LAPEER, Mich., Oct 19 - 

Durakon Industries Inc said it has entered into a definitive agreement to acquire DFM Corp, a maker of bug and gravel protective shields for trucks and cars, for an undisclosed amount of cash and debentures, retroactive to September One.  Reuter &#3;
