ROBINSON NUGENT <RNIC.O> PLANT HAS FIRE

    NEW ALBANY, Ind., June 2 - 

Robinson Nugent Inc said a fire that started in the electroplating operation damaged its Delemont, Switzerland, plant.     The company said cleanup operations are in progress and it will serve customer requirments from other plants during the period of cleanup and equipment repair or replacement.  Reuter &#3;
