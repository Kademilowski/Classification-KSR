TEXAS AIR <TEX> UNIT TO ADD MEXICO FLIGHTS

    ATLANTA, March 9 - 

Texas Air Corp's Continental Airlines unit said it will begin service to Mexico from Atlanta in May.     Continental said it will add non-stop flights to Mexico City, Puerto Vallarta, Cancun, Acapulco and Ixtapa.  Reuter &#3;
