XIDEX <XIDX.O> NAMES NEW CHIEF EXECUTIVE

    PALO ALTO, Calif., Oct 19 - 

Xidex Corp said Lester L. Colbert Jr. will be succeeded as president and chief executive officer by executive vice president and chief operating officer Bert Zaccaria but will remain chairman.     The company said Colbert has decided to take a less active role in management.  Reuter &#3;
