PAULEY PETROLEUM <PP> NAMES NEWGARD PRESIDENT

    LOS ANGELES, April 13 - 

Pauley Petroleum Inc said it appointed Mark Newgard president and chief operating officer.     Newgard, 42, had been president of privately-held Edgington Oil Co.     He succeeds William Pagen, Pauley's chairman, who had also been serving as president, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
