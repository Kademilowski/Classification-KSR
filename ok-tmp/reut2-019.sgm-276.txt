JAPAN BUYS 8,000 TONNES OF CANADIAN RAPESEED

    WINNIPEG, June 19 - 

Japanese crushers bought 8,000 tonnes of Canadian rapeseed for shipment from the last-half of July to September, trade sources said.     Price details were not available.  Reuter &#3;
