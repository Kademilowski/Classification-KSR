CENTURY BANCORP FILES FOR STOCK OFFERING

    SOMERVILLE, Mass., April 8 - 

<Century Bancorp Inc> said it filed for an initial public offering of 1.6 mln shares of Class A common shares through an underwriting group managed by Moseley Securities Corp.     The company said the Class A shares are non-voting except under certain limited conditions.  Reuter &#3;
