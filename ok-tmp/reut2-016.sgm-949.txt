SPAIN'S CONSUMER PRICES RISE 0.6 PCT IN MARCH

    MADRID, April 21 - 

Spain's consumer price index rose 0.6 pct in March after a 0.4 pct increase in February and 0.3 pct in March last year, the National Statistics Institute said.     The March rise brought the year on year rate to 6.3 pct. The government has set a five pct target for 1987 consumer price inflation after an 8.3 pct increase last year.  REUTER &#3;
