NEW YORK FUTURES EXCHANGE TO RELOCATE

    NEW YORK, April 3 - 

Lewis Horowitz, president of the New York Futures Exchange, said the New York Stock Exchange board yesterday approved in principle an agreement to relocate the futures exchange at the New York Cotton Exchange.     The agreement is subject to the approval of the Cotton Exchange board, which meets Tuesday, April 7.  Reuter &#3;
