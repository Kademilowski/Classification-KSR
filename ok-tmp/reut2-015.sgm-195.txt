ORANGE-CO <OJ> HOLDERS APPROVE NAME CHANGE

    BLOOMFIELD HILLS, Mich., April 8 - 

Orange-Co Inc said shareholders at the annual meeting approved a name change to Stoneridge Resources Inc.     The company said yesterday shareholders of American Agronomics Corp <AGR>, which acquired Orange-Co's Orange-Co of Florida subsidiary late in 1986, approved a name change to Orange-Co Inc.
