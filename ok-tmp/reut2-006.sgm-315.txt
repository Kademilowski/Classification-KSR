UNITED TECHNOLOGIES <UTX> GETS NAVY CONTRACT

    WASHINGTON, March 17 - 

The Sikorsky Aircraft division of United Technologies Corp is being awarded a 27.2 mln dlr contract modification for four Combat Search and Rescue Special Warfare Support helicopters, the Defense Department said.     It said work on the contract is expected to be completed in December 1992.  Reuter &#3;
