COMMUNITY BANK <CBSI> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

    SYRACUSE, N.Y., March 3 - 

Community Bank System Inc said it has entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Nichols Community Bank for 2,800,000 dlrs in common stock.     It said subject to approval by Nichols shareholders and regulatory authorities, the transaction is expected to be completed later this year.  Reuter &#3;
