PACIFICARE <PHSY> IN TALKS TO ACQUIRE HMO

    CYPRESS, Calif., March 23 - 

PacifiCare Health Systems Inc said it is in negotiations to acquire Capital Health Care, a 40,000 member health maintenance organization servicing Salem and Corvallis, Ore.     The company said it will not disclose terms or other details of the acquisition until negotiations are completed.   Reuter &#3;
