TOYOTA ANNOUNCES HIGHER DOMESTIC CAR SALES

    TORRANCE, Calif., April 3 - 

Toyota Motor Sales U.S.A. Inc said domestic car sales for the month of march rose four pct to 40,554 units from 39,008, but truck sales were down 19.3 pct to 28,046, compared with the 34,745 sold in March 1986.  Reuter &#3;
