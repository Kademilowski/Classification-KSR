ICO <ICO> TO BE DELISTED FROM AMEX

    FORT WORTH, Texas, March 6 - 

ICO Inc said its common stock will be removed from trading on the American Stock Exchange because it failed to meet certain exchange guidelines, including net worth requirements.     The company, which has been unprofitable for three out of the last four years, said its stock will be delisted from the AMEX on March 13 and will begin trading on the Boston Stock Exchange on March 16.
