ALSTHOM AWARDED 900 MLN FRANC PAKISTANI DEAL

    PARIS, March 26 - 

French heavy engineering group Alsthom <ALSF.PA> has won a 900 mln franc contract from Pakistan to supply four 100 MW gas turbines, the company said in a press release.     The turbines are for the electricity generating plant at Kot-Addu, in central Pakistan and are expected to be operational at the end of 1988.  REUTER &#3;
