FEDERAL PAPERBOARD <FBO> ENTERS AGREEMENT

    MONTVALE, N.J., April 8 - 

Federal Paperboard Co Inc said it has entered into an agreement with Ozaki Trading Co Ltd of Osaka, Japan, allowing Ozaki to represent Federal Paperboard's bleached paperboard sales in Japan.     Federal added it will be opening an office in Japan in the near future.      Reuter &#3;
