INSTINET <INET> CHANGES SPECIAL MEETING DATE

    NEW YORK, March 27 - 

Instinet Corp said the special meeting at which shareholders will vote on its proposed merger with Reuters Holdings PLC <RTRSY> will be held May 12.     Instinet said it had previously scheduled the meeting for May 21.     It said the record date for the meeting remains April 10.  Reuter &#3;
