AMERICAN PRECISION <APR>, WORKERS HAVE AGREEMENT

    BUFFALO, N.Y., April 3 - 

American Precision Industries Inc said members of United Auto Workers Local 1416 and the company's Delevan and Deltran divisions reached agreement on a three-year contract.     The company provided no details other than the contract is effective from March 29, 1987, through March 31, 1990.  Reuter &#3;
