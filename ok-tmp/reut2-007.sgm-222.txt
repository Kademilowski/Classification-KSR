CHAMPION <CHA> IN 285 MLN DLR EXPANSION

    STAMFORD, Conn, March 19 - 

Champion International Corp said its board approved a 285 mln dlrs expansion of its mill in Hinton, Alberta, Canada.     The project will double capacity of the mill's pulping operation to 424,000 short tonnes a year.     Champion expects the expansion to be completed by 1989.  Reuter &#3;
