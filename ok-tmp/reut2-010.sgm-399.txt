FCS LABS <FCSI> PRESIDENT, TREASURER RESIGN

    FLEMINGTON, N.J., March 27 - 

FCS Laboratories Inc said George D. Fowle Jr has resigned as president of the company and Garu Griffin resigned as treasurer, both effective March 31.     The company said directors elected Chairman Nicholas A. Gallo III to the additional title of president and Chief Financial Officer Richard D. Mayo was elected treasurer.  Reuter &#3;
