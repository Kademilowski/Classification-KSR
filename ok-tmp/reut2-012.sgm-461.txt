SYRIA SEEKING WHITE SUGAR ON APRIL 8 - TRADE

    LONDON, April 2 - 

Syria will hold a buying tender on April 8 for 36,000 tonnes of white sugar for shipment in June, July and August at a rate of 12,000 tonnes a month, traders said.  REUTER &#3;
