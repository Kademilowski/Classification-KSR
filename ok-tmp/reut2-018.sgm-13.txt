ICCO BUYS 5,000 TONNES COCOA FOR BUFFER STOCK

    LONDON, June 2 - 

The International Cocoa Organization (ICCO) buffer stock manager bought 5,000 tonnes of cocoa today for the buffer stock, traders said.     The cocoa is believed to have been entirely made up of second hand material, they added.     Such a purchase would bring cumulative buffer stock purchases to 26,000 tonnes.  Reuter &#3;
