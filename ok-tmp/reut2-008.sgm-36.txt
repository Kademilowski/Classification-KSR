GUEST SUPPLY <GEST> GETS SHAREHOLDER SUIT

    NORTH BRUNSWICK, N.J., March 20 - 

Guest Supply INc said a shareholder sued it and certain officers and directors, alleging misrepresentations and omissions in the prospectus for the company's September 1986 public offering.     Guest said the complaint is without merit and will defend the action vigorously.  Reuter &#3;
