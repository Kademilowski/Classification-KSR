CERMETEK <CRMK> INCREASES WORK WEEK

    SUNNYVALE, Calif., March 30 - 

Cermetek Microelectronics Inc said it will return to a full 40 hour work week, efective at the end of this month, as a result of increased business.     Last October Cermetek, a maker of modems, data base access and communications terminals, reduced its work week to 36 hours to reduce costs.  Reuter &#3;
