SABENA BEGINS BRUSSELS-TORONTO SERVICE

    Toronto, March 30 - 

Sabena Belgian World Airlines said it began today three-times-weekly service between Brussels and Toronto.     Sabena said it will fly combined passenger-freight flights non-stop into Toronto on Mondays and Thursdays. Sunday flights will stop in Montreal.  Reuter &#3;
