ERICSSON WINS 400 MLN CROWN ALGERIAN ORDER

    STOCKHOLM, March 24 - 

L M Ericsson <ERIC ST> said it had signed a contract worth nearly 400 mln crowns with the Algerian Ministry of Telecommunications covering the supply of AXE exchanges as part of an expansion of the country's telecommunications network.  REUTER &#3;
