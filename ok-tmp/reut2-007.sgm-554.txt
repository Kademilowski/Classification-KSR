GERMAN M3 RISES 2.6 BILLION MARKS IN FEBRUARY

    FRANKFURT, March 20 - 

West German M3 money supply rose a seasonally adjusted 2.6 billion marks in February to 1,035.1 billion, the Bundesbank said.     The rise compares with a revised 14.8 billion mark increase in January and a 2.2 billion rise in February 1986.  REUTER &#3;
