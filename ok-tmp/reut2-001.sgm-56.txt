SMALL QUANTITY OF UK WHEAT SOLD TO HOME MARKET

    LONDON, March 3 - 

A total of 2,435 tonnes of British intervention feed wheat were sold at today's tender for the home market out of requests for 3,435 tonnes, the Home Grown Cereals Authority, HGCA, said.     Price details were not reported.     No bids were submitted for intervention barley.  Reuter &#3;
