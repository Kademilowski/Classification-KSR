GOULD <GLD> REGISTERS TO OFFER PREFERRED

    ROLLING MEADOWS, Ill., March 5 - 

Gould Inc said it registered to offer two mln shares of convertible exchangeable preferred stock.     Gould said the proposed new issue will be underwritten by First Boston Corp and Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc.     In a registration statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Gould said the shares will be convertible at any time into Gould common.     The shares also will be exchangeable into Gould convertible subordinated debentures at a rate of 50 dlrs principal amount per debenture.  Reuter &#3;
