JORDAN TENDERING FOR U.S. WHEAT - EXPORTERS

    KANSAS CITY, April 2 - 

Jordan will tender Monday for 225,000 tonnes of U.S. hard and soft wheat under the USDA's Export Bonus program for April through November shipment, private export sources said.  Reuter &#3;
