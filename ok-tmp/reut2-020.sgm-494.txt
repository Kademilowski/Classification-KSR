EXOVIR <XOVR.O> TO BUYBACK SHARES

    GREAT NECK, N.Y., Oct 20 - 

Exovir Inc said its board authorized the purchase of up to one mln dlrs of its common stock.     The dollar amount represents approximately 120,000 shares, the company said. Exovir has about three mln common shares outstanding.     The company said it believed its stock was substantially undervalued.  Reuter &#3;
