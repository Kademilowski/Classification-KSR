U.K. MONEY MARKET GIVEN 550 MLN STG LATE HELP

    LONDON, March 16 - 

The Bank of England said it provided the money market with late assistance of around 550 mln stg.     This takes the Bank's total help today to some 1.12 billion stg and compares with its estimated deficit of around 1.10 billion.  REUTER &#3;
