ALLTEL <AT> GETS PERMISSION FOR LICENSE TRANSFER

ALLTEL Corp said it has received permission from the Federal Communications Commission for the transfer of its Syracuse/Binghamton/Cortland, N.Y., radio paging licenses to Mobile Communications Corp of America <MCCABV.O> and the transfer takes effect today.  Reuter &#3;
