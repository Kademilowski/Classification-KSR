BANK OF FRANCE LEAVES INTERVENTION RATE UNCHANGED

    PARIS, April 2 - 

The Bank of France said it left its intervention rate unchanged at 7-3/4 pct when it injected funds to the market against first category paper in todays money market intervention tender.     Money market dealers had earlier expressed mixed views on a possible quarter point cut.     The rate was last adjusted on March 9, when it was reduced to 7-3/4 pct from the eight pct rate set in January.  REUTER &#3;
