CANNON INT'L <CAN> SETS PHILIPPINES FILM PACT

    LOS ANGELES, April 9 - 

Cannon International said <Viva Films International> agreed to exclusively distribute Cannon films in the Philippines for the new four years.     The company said the agreement covers both theatrical and video distribution. Terms were not disclosed.   Reuter &#3;
