CEREALS MCAS TO BE UNCHANGED NEXT WEEK

    BRUSSELS, April 1 - 

Monetary compensatory amounts, MCA's, will be unchanged for the week starting April 6, EC Commission officials said.     Cereals MCA's are plus 2.4 points for West Germany and the Netherlands, minus two points for Denmark, minus eight points for France, minus nine for Ireland, minus 5.7 for Italy, minus 25.7 for Britain, minus 44.1 for Greece and minus 10.5 for Spain.
