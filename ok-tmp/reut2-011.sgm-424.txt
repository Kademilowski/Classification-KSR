FED ADDS RESERVES VIA CUSTOMER REPURCHASES

    NEW YORK, March 31 - 

The Federal Reserve entered the U.S. Government securities market to arrange 1.5 billion dlrs of customer repurchase agreements, a Fed spokesman said.
