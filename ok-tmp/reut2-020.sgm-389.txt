ALLIED IRISH BANKS OPENS TOKYO OFFICE

    LONDON, Oct 20- 

Allied Irish Banks Plc <ALBK.L> said it plans to open a Tokyo office.     Initially it will be a representative office but the company said it hopes to have a full banking licence in about two years.  REUTER &#3;
