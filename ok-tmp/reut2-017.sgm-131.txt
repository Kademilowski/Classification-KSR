JAPAN BUYS UP TO 8,000 TONNES CANADIAN RAPESEED

    WINNIPEG, April 24 - 

Japanese crushers bought 5,000 to 8,000 tonnes of Canadian rapeseed for last-half May/first-half June shipment, trade sources said. Price details were not available.  Reuter &#3;
