PRESIDENTIAL AIRWAYS <PAIR> PACT APPROVED

    WASHINGTON, FEB 26 - 

Presidential Airways Inc said its joint marketing and services agreement with Texas Air Corp's <TXN> Continental Airlines unit was approved by the U.S. Department of Justice.     According to the agreement, Presidential Airways will operate scheduled service under the name "Continental Express." The company, however, will remain independent.      Reuter &#3;
