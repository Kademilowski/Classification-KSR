BANKING CENTER <TBCX> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

    WATERBURY, Conn., March 16 - 

Banking Center said it has signed a letter of intent to acquire First Railroad Mortgage Co from First Union Bank of Augusta, Ga., for undisclosed terms.     The company said First Railroad had loan production of over 100 mln dlrs in 1986 and was servicing over 435 mln dlrs in loans at the end of 1986.  Reuter &#3;
