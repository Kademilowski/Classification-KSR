WALKER TELECOMMUNICTIONS <WTEL> FIBER TO START

    HAUPPAUGE, N.Y., March 23 - 

Walker Telecommunications Corp said the second phase of a 417-mile fiber optics network in Michigan will begin operating March 30.     The first phase was completed in February and connects Grand Rapids with Detroit via Lansing.     The 31 mln dlr network will link 14 Michigan cities, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
