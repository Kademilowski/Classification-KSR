    ATLANTA, Ga, March 19 - 

The American Stock Exchange said it has approved the listing of 12.3 mln common shares of Landmark Technology Corp.     Trading of the shares will begin Friday, March 20. The stock is currently traded over the counter under the symbol LTCO.  Reuter &#3;
