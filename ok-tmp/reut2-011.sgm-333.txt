NORD RESOURCES <NRD> SELLS SHARES PRIVATELY

    DAYTON, Ohio, March 31 - 

Nord Resources Corp said it has agreed to make a private placement of 600,000 common shares to institutional investors in Britain and elsewhere outside the U.S. priced at 17.50 dlrs per share.     It said funds will be used for general corporate purposes.     Closing of the placement is scheduled for April 10, Nord said.  Reuter &#3;
