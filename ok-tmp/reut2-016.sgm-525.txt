NWA <NWA> AIRLINE ADDS TRANSPACIFIC FLIGHT

NWA Inc said its Northwest Airlines unit will add a nonstop Detroit to Tokyo flight April 17.     The new flight will operate three times a week until June one, when it will fly seven days a week.  Reuter &#3;
