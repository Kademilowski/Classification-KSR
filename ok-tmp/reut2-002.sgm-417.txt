NORTHGATE <NGX> NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

    TORONTO, March 5 - 

Northgate Exploration Ltd said it appointed John Kearney as president of the company, effective immediately.     A company spokesman said Patrick Hughes, who previously was both president and chairman, will continue as chairman and chief executive officer.     Kearney had been executive vice-president of Northgate.  Reuter &#3;
