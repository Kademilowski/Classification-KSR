E-SYSTEMS <ESY> WINS NAVY CONTRACT

    DALLAS, March 17 - 

E-Systems said it received a multi-year U.S. Navy contract for the continuing production of its military teleprinters for use in submarines.     It said the contract is worth 9,208,223 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
