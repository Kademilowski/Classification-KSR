JENNIFER CONVERTIBLES <JENN>INITIAL OFFER STARTS

    NEW YORK, March 12 - 

Jennifer Convertibles Inc said an initial public offering of 500,000 units is underway at eight dlrs each through underwriters led by Evans and Co Inc.     Each unit consists of two common shares and one redeemable Class A warrant enabling the holder to buy one common share at five dlrs until March 11, 1992.     The shares and warrants are immediately separately transferable.  Reuter &#3;
