U.K. MONEY MARKET SURPLUS REVISED TO 250 MLN STG

    LONDON, March 25 - 

The Bank of England said it revised up its forecast of today's surplus in the money markets to 250 mln stg from its earlier estimate of a 150 mln.     The central bank has not operated in the market today.  REUTER &#3;
