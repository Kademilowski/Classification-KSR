CURRENCIES BETTER REFLECT FUNDAMENTALS - BAKER

    MIAMI, March 23 - 

Treasury Secertary James Baker said currencies were now within ranges that better reflected economic fundamentals.     In a speech to the annual meeting of the Inter-American Development Bank, he said, "Excchange have moved into ranges that better reflect economic fundamentals."
