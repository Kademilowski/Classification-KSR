GERBER <GEB> SETS DEADLINE FOR UNIT'S BUYOUT

    FREMONT, MICH., April 3 - 

Gerber Products Co said it has given management of its CWT Inc trucking subsidiary 60 days to pursue a leveraged buyout of the subsidiary.     It said CWT Inc, which has operations in the Midwest and Southeast, has annual revenues of approximately 135 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
