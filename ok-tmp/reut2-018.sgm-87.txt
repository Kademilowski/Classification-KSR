NEW YORK LIFE OFFERING A GLOBAL MUTUAL FUND

    NEW YORK, June 2 - 

<New York Life Insurance Co> said it is offering its first global mutual fund through its wholly-owned subsidiary, MacKay-Shields Financial Corp.     The new fund is called the MacKay-Shields Global Fund, the company said. Its portfolio contains foreign and U.S. securities, the company said.      Reuter &#3;
