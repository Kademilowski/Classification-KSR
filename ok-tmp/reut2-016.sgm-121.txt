CHINA SULPHUR-IRON MINE STARTS PRODUCTION

    PEKING, April 13 - 

China's largest sulphur-iron mine has started trial production at Yunfu in the southern province of Guangdong, the China Daily said.     It said the mine has an annual output capacity of three mln tonnes of sulphur-iron ore, which can be used without processing because of its high quality.  REUTER &#3;
