ACTON <ATN> SETS ONE FOR FIVE REVERSE SPLIT

    ACTON, Mass, June 18 - 

Acton Corp said shareholders at the annaul meeting approved a one-for-five reverse split that takes effect June 25.     It said holders also approved a limitation of directors' liability and indemnity agreements between the company and its officers and directors.  Reuter &#3;
