ARNOX <ARNX.O> STILL IN MERGER TALKS WITH TXL

    GREENWICH, Conn., Oct 19 - 

Arnox Corp said a special committee of its board is continuing talks with <TXL Corp> on the terms of TXL's offer to acquire Arnox at 10 dlrs per share.     It said TXL has arranged preliminary financing.  Reuter &#3;
