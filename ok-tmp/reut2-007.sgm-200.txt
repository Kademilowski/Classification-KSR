MORTGAGE AND REALTY <MRT> ELECTS NEW PRESIDENT

    ELKINS PARK, Penn., March 19 - 

Mortgage and Realty Trust said C.W. Strong Jr., executive vice president, will succeed Hayward L. Elliott as president and chief executive officer when Elliott retires on April 1987.     It said Elliott, 70, will remain a trustee.  Reuter &#3;
