PANSOPHIC SYSTEMS <PNS> SPLITS STOCK 2-FOR-1

    OAK BROOK, Ill., March 3 - 

Pansophic Systems Inc said it will split its stock two-for-one effective April two to shareholders of record March 13.     It also said it will pay a six cts per share dividend on the pre-split shares, a regular quarterly dividend, on April two to shareholders of record March 13.  Reuter &#3;
