EC REJECTS WHEAT EXPORT BIDS, GRANTS BARLEY

    BRUSSELS, March 5 - 

The European Community's cereal management committee rejected all bids to export free market soft wheat at today's weekly tender, traders said.     The committee awarded 123,000 tonnes of free market barley export licences at a maximum export refund of 138.75 Ecus per tonne. &#3;
