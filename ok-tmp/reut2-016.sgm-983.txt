BOMB BLAST ROCKS WHITE AREA OF SOUTH AFRICA

    JOHANNESBURG, April 21 - 

A bomb rocked a white area of Johannesburg but first reports said no one was injured.     Police said an explosive device was placed under a car in the Mayfair district.  Reuter &#3;
