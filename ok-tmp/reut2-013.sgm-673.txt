SIOUX CITY HOGS SEEN UP 0.50 DLR - PRIVATE

    Sioux City, April 7 - Sunny/34 degrees - 

Hog prices expected 0.50 dlr higher on estimated receipts of 3,200 head, private sources said. Top seen at 51.00 dlrs per cwt.     Sources cited higher cut-out values late yesterday.  Reuter &#3;
