FED NOT EXPECTED TO TAKE MONEY MARKET ACTION

    NEW YORK, June 2 - 

The Federal Reserve is not expected to intervene in the government securities market to add or drain reserves at its usual intervention time this morning, economists said.     With the Federal funds rate trading comfortably at  6-9/16 pct, down from yesterday's 6.74 pct average, economists said the Fed did not need to take reserve management action today.  Reuter &#3;
