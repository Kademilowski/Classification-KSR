BUNDESBANK WILL NOT HOLD PRESS CONFERENCE TOMORROW

    FRANKFURT, March 18 - 

The Bundesbank will not hold a press conference after its regular fortnightly council meeting tomorrow, a spokeswoman said in answer to enquiries.     Bundesbank President Karl Otto Poehl will chair the meeting.     The next meeting is scheduled for April 2.  REUTER &#3;
