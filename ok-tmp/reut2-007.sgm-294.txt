LANSON NAMES WHITBREAD PLC UNIT AS IMPORTER

    NEW YORK, March 19 - 

Lanson Champagne of France said it named Whitbread PLC's Regal Brands Inc unit as its exclusive U.S. importer, effective April one.     Regal Brands is also the sole U.S. importer for Benedictine S.A.'s liqueurs.  Reuter &#3;
