FRENCH CAISSE NATIONALE DES AUTOROUTES ISSUES BOND

    PARIS, March 19 - 

Caisse Nationale des Autoroutes is issuing a three billion French franc bond, the French bond issuing committee said.     The issue will be lead-managed by Caisse Nationale du Credit Agricole, Credit Lyonnais and Cie Financiere de Paribas subsidiary Credit du Nord.  REUTER &#3;
