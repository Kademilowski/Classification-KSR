PORTUGAL MAY HAVE PURCHASED U.S. CORN

    CHICAGO, March 24 - 

Portugal may have purchased a 30,000 tonne cargo at its tender today for up to 43,000 tonnes of number two yellow corn (14.5 pct maximum moisture) for arrival by April 30, shipment via Gulf ports, U.S. exporters said.  Reuter &#3;
