HEEKIN CAN <HEKN> TO BUILD OHIO PLANT

    CINCINNATI, March 12 - 

Heekin Can Inc said it plans to build a seven mln dlr Columbus, Ohio, plant financed by industrial revenue bonds.     It said construction on the 50,000 square foot plant is scheduled to start in April and manufacturing will start at the end of 1987.  The plant will initially make two-piece 6.5 ounce steel cans for pet food.  Reuter &#3;
