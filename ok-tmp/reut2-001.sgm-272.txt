USDA REPORTS CORN SOLD SOLD TO UNKNOWN

    WASHINGTON, March 3 - 

The U.S. Agriculture Department said private U.S. exporters reported sales of 104,000 tonnes of corn to unknown destinations for shipment in the 1986/87 marketing year.     The marketing year for corn began September 1, it said.  Reuter &#3;
