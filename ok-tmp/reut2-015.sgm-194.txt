RESEARCH INDUSTRIES <REIC> UNVEILS NEW PRODUCTS

    SALT LAKE CITY, April 8 - 

Research Industries Corp said it is introducing three additions to its line of cardiovascular specialty products.     The company said the products are a patented drainage bicaval cannula for open heart surgery, a proprietary catheter for neonatal thoracic surgery and a line of specialized baloon catheter shunts for carotid artery surgery.
