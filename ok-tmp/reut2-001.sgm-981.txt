SWISS OFFERS NEW SERIES OF MONEY MARKET PAPER

    ZURICH, March 5 - 

The Swiss Finance Ministry is inviting tenders for a new series of three-month money market certificates to raise about 150 mln Swiss francs, the Swiss National Bank said.     Bids would be due on March 10 and payment on March 12.     The last issue of three-month paper conducted on February 12 yielded 2.969 pct.  REUTER &#3;
