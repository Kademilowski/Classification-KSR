SMARTNAMES TO BUY AMERICAN INFORMATION NETWORK

    WALTHAM, Mass., March 13 - 

SmartNames Inc said it reached an agreement in principle to buy American Information Network.     Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.     The company said the purchase of American will allow it to meet the needs of a broader range of direct mail industry customers.  Reuter &#3;
