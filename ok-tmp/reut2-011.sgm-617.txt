MEDIQ <MED> UNIT IN CONTRACT

    PENNSAUKEN, N.J., March 31 - 

Mediq Inc said its Computer Health Corp unit has contracted with the Florida State Employees Health Insurance Plan to provide mobile health exams to about 100,000 employees and their spouses.     Mediq also said it acquired Hexam Inc a unit of Kraft Inc <KRA> for undisclosed terms.  Reuter &#3;
