PRIME COMPUTER <PRM> TO BUY BACK STOCK

    NATICK, Mass., Oct 20 - 

Prime Computer Inc said it will periodically repurchase an unspecified number of its common stock on the open market.     The company has 49.1 mln shares outstanding.     It said it stock will be bought in periods of price weakness and that the stock will be used for the company's restricted stock and employee stock purchase plans and for other corporate purposes.
