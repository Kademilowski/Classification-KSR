ROY F. WESTON WINS GOVERNMENT CONTRACT

    WEST CHESTER, Pa., March 27 - 

Roy F. Weston Inc said it was awarded a three-year contract valued at three mln dlrs to provide technical assistance to the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.     The company said it is a subcontractor to Sanford Cohen and Associates of McLean, Va.  Reuter &#3;
