SMITH INTERNATIONAL <SII> CONSOLIDATES DIVISIONS

    NEWPORT BEACH, Calif., March 18 - 

Smith International Inc said it has consolidated its Smith Tool and Smith Drilling Systems divisions.     The new division will be called Smith International and will be headquartered in Houston.     Doug Rock, previously president of Smith Tool, is now president of Smith International.  Reuter &#3;
