ANALYSIS/TECHNOLOGY <AATI> WINS NAVY CONTRACTS

    NORTH STONINGTON, Conn., March 6 - 

Analysis and Technology Inc said it won contracts and increases in existing contracts valued at 2.9 mln dlrs from the U.S. Navy in February.     The awards include 1.8 mln dlrs an extension of an existing contract for engineering and technical services and 1.1 mln dlrs in several smaller contracts.  Reuter &#3;
