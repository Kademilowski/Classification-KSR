RAYTHEON <RTN> DIRECTOR RESIGNS

    LEXINGTON, Mass., March 25 - 

Raytheon Co said D. Brainerd Holmes, who retired as Raytheon president in May 1986, has resigned from the board effective April One.     The company said Ferdinand Colloredo-Mansfield, chairman of Cabot, Cabot and Forbes, will succeed Holmes on the board.  Reuter &#3;
