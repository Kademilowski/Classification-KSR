JAPAN BUYS 4,000 TONNES OF CANADIAN RAPESEED

    WINNIPEG, April 21 - 

Japan bought 4,000 tonnes of Canadian rapeseed for last-half May/first-half June shipment, nearly completing buying for May needs, trade sources said.      Price details were not available.   Reuter &#3;
