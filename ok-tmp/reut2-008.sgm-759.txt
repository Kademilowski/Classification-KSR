U.K. MONEY MARKET GIVEN 10 MLN STG LATE ASSISTANCE

    LONDON, March 24 - 

The Bank of England said it provided the money market with late help of about 10 mln stg.     This takes the bank's total help today to some 229 mln stg and compares with its forecast of a 300 mln stg shortage.  REUTER &#3;
