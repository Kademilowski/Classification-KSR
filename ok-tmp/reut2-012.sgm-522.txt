U.K. MONEY MARKET GIVEN 40 MLN STG LATE HELP

    LONDON, April 2 - 

The Bank of England said it had provided the money market with around 40 mln stg late assistance. This takes the Bank's total help today to some 537 mln stg and compares with its estimate of a 700 mln stg shortage.  REUTER &#3;
