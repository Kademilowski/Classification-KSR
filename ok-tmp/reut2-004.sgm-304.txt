INDIA BUYS RBD PALM OLEIN AT TENDER

    LONDON, March 12 - 

The Indian State Trading Corp bought one, or possibly two, 6,000 tonne cargoes of Malaysian refined bleached deodorised palm olein at its vegetable oil import tender today for Mar 15/Apr 15 shipment at 355 dlrs per tonne cif, traders said.  Reuter &#3;
