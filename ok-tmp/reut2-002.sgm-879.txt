TECHNIGEN PLATINUM CORP IN METALS FIND

    VANCOUVER, March 6 - 

Technigen Platinum corp said initial results of a 13-hole drilling program on its R.M. Nicel platinum property in Rouyn-Noranda, Quebec, indicate extensive near-surface zones highly enriched in gold, platinum and palladium.     The metals were found in rocks on the periphery of a sulphide deposit.     It said values of up to 0.073 ounce of platinum, 0.206 ounce palladium, three pct copper and 4.5 pct nickel were found over a drill section of 13 feet.  Reuter &#3;
