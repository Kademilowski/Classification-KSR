CLEVITE <CLEV> FORMS JOINT VENTURE

    GLENVIEW, ILL., March 16 - 

Clevite Industries Inc said it agreed in principle to form a joint venture company with Bridgestone Corp of Tokyo.     Based in Milan, Ohio, the joint venture company, Clevite-Bridgestone Co, will manufacture and sell rubber-to-metal products for the automotive industry in the United States, Canada and Mexico.     Initially, it said the new company will manufacture products in Clevite's existing Angola, Ind. plant.   Reuter &#3;
