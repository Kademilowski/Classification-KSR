TANDEM COMPUTERS <TNDM> GETS INSTALLATION

Tandem Computers Inc said Pacific Gas and Electric Co <PCG> installed a Tandem computer system for on-line information management at the Diablo Canyon nuclear power plant near San Luis Obispo, Calif.     No value for installation of the Nonstop VLX system was given.  Reuter &#3;
