NAPCO <NPCO> MANAGEMENT CANNOT RAISE FUNDING

    HOPKINS, MINN., April 9 - 

Napco International Inc said it has suspended its plan to sell its international business to a group of that business' top managers because the group has failed to obtain satisfactory financing.     The company also said it still intends to pursue a new corporate direction, and is exploring acquisition alternatives.  Reuter &#3;
