CARGILL U.K. STRIKE TALKS TO RESUME THIS AFTERNOON

    LONDON, March 25 - 

Talks between Cargill U.K. Ltd's management and unions, aimed at ending the prolonged strike at its Seaforth oilseed processing plant, will resume this afternoon, a company spokesman said.     Yesterday's session failed to reach a compromise but some progress was made, he said.  REUTER &#3;
