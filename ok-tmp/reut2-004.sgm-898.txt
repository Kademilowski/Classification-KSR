AMERICAN HONDA HAS HIGHER EARLY MARCH SALES

    GARDENA, Calif., March 13 - 

<American Honda Motor Co Inc> said its domestic sales for the first 10 days of March totaled 4,394 as compared to 3,786 a year earlier.     It said year-to-date sales rose to 52,143 from 31,686 last year.  Reuter &#3;
