PHARMACIA <PHAB.O> ESTABLISHES VENTURE FUND

    PISCATAWAY, N.J., June 2 - 

Pharmacia Inc's Pharmacia Develpoment Co Inc said it established Pharmacia Ventures Inc to make investments in U.S. growth companies.     It said investments will be made in such firms as those producing in-vitro diagnostics, biotechnology, ophthalmology and health care.  Reuter &#3;
