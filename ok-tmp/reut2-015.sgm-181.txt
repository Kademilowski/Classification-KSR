ARVIN <ARV>, SANGO START BUILDING PLANT

    MADISON, Ind., April 8 - 

Arvin Industries Inc said it and Sango Co Ltd of Japan have started building a new 119,000 square foot joint venture auto exhaust plant in Madison, Wis.     The company said the 15.2 mln dlr plant of its Arvin Sango Inc venture with Sango is scheduled to be in operation early in 1988.  Reuter &#3;
