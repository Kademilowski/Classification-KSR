NEW JERSEY HOSPITAL ASSOCIATION SYMPOSIUM

    PRINCETON, April 8 - 

The New Jersey Hospital Association said it will hold a symposium on the quality of hospital care on Friday, April 10.     Thirty-eight health-care executives from 18 nations will be on hand at the symposium.   Reuter &#3;
